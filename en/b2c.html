B2C Business Solution

The "thread of reasoning" on why traditional CRMs are no longer enough.

From Data Collection to 1:1 Engagement

The goal of engaging the Person in a value exchange (consensual profiling for personalization) is unattainable with traditional methods.

If it were possible, your CRMs and your cloud AI platforms would already be doing it.

The problem is that those tools were designed to *take pictures*, not to *motivate the Person to get to know you better in exchange for better service*. They cannot, as designed, put the Person at the center of your company.

To meet the 1:1 needs of the individual, you must flip the model: stop "collecting" and start "engaging." This requires a radically new Platform.

CtD is necessary: the only platform designed and developed to do this.

Instead of collecting and managing your customers' sensitive data on your servers, CtD's intelligence operates *on the Person's device and with their full awareness*, creating the conditions for:

  • A Transparent Dialogue: The Person is incentivized to describe their needs in detail, because they have a technical guarantee that this data *remains theirs*.
  • Dynamic Personalization of Products and Services: Your business can send "logics" and "offers" processed locally by CtD, generating a tailor-made solution *in real time*.
  • An Architecture Built on Trust: Loyalty is no longer a slogan, but a demonstrable technical feature of the service.

How We Operate (B2C)

  1. Strategic Assessment: We analyze your current GTM and identify the breaking points in your customer relationships.
  2. Rapid Prototyping (POC): We integrate CtD with your systems (see Specialized Services) and launch a pilot project on a customer segment to measure the ROI of trust.
  3. Rollout and Optimization: Once the model is validated, we define the rollout strategy for the entire customer base and optimize data flows.
